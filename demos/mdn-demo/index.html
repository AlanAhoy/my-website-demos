How I met your mother

HIMYM's wikipedia.

How I Met Your Mother (or HIMYM) is an American situation comedy (sitcom) that started on CBS Broadcasting on September 19, 2005.

The story is set in New York City. It is about a man named Ted Mosby who tells his son and daughter how he met their mother. Ted is played by Josh Radnor and the story is narrated by Bob Saget who plays Ted Mosby in the future year 2030. Other characters and friends of Ted are:

  • Marshall Eriksen (Jason Segel)
  • Robin Scherbatsky (Cobie Smulders)
  • Barney Stinson (Neil Patrick Harris)
  • Lily Aldrin (Alyson Hannigan)

"How I Met Your Mother" is also said to be the inheritor of "Friends" -- another famous sitcom from 1994 to 2004.
